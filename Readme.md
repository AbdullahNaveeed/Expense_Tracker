# 💰 Personal Expense Tracker

A **command-line application** to track personal expenses, built with **Python** and **OOP principles**.  
Keep track of your spending, categories, and monthly summaries with ease!

---

## ✨ Features

- 📝 **Add Expenses:** Record expenses with name, amount, and category  
- 💾 **Persistent Storage:** Data saved in CSV for future sessions  
- 📊 **View Spending by Category:** Analyze your spending and detect overspending  
- 📆 **Monthly Summary:** Get a snapshot of your monthly expenses  
- 💰 **Balance Tracking:** Detect debts and track your remaining balance  

---
